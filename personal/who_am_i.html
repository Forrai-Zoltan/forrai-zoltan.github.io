About the Website

It was at the quiet wake of the great pestilence, when time was shackled and stood still, when people were imprisoned by an edict of solitude. Therein, I envisioned a place, immaterial in its essence, carven by words and built by spells. I conceived it as a library, a place to trace and revisit my notes on writing and wizardry. Since then, I greatly expanded it with other pages. And yet, the work never ends, ever do I discover areas to amend and changes to make.

I erected this edifice to liberate myself from three vices of the modern web:

  1. To evade the artificial shroud of social media’s voyeuristic surveillance.
  2. To elude the squalling squibs of transgressive propaganda and the vouchsafed ads that burden with debt-laden waste.
  3. To escape the fine-tuned pleasure machine that chisels your mental marble into a gratified gravestone of volition.

What you can expect

First and foremost, this is a literary website.
It is not a “book blog” although I do review books. Not a “writer’s portfolio,” although I do self-publish my works on here. And certainly not a plain weblog either, although, again, I do share essays and update a micro-blog. No, this website is an extension of me, a finely coded fingerprint of the self.

As you can read on every page, I’m a full time writer not. My schedule is awash with excess tasks and other responsibilities. And I cannot yet commit all my time to literary labour. My lettered yield is yet sporadic, but I wish to gift a tale with every turn of the moon. Later perhaps, every week. So, if you savour this style of writing, I can promise to continue with my relentlessly purple prose and perfumed poetry, and provide you with the weirdest of fiction, free of charge.

A second note; since I don’t participate in social media this website is my primary way to share updates. My warbles page has an RSS feed you can follow. Or you can find me on NewGrounds. I will not do a newsletter, I find them to be the epitome of cringe.

Website Guide A-> N

A good website is designed like a good maze. It allows you to get lost and wander, but also rewards you when you do find your way.
I did, however, provide a small legend for you here to orientate yourself.

  • Warbles -> A micro-blog with an RSS feed for short thoughts and updates.
  • Library -> My personal library where I track what I’ve read or want to read.
  • Contact -> A contact form to reach me directly.
  • Patronage -> I hope to write full-time one day, but I’ve yet to earn the right to ask anything for my words. Support someone more deserving.
  • Shop -> Same as above. Eventually I would like to sell prints.
  • Colophone -> The twin page to this one. The Recto to the Verso.
  • Decks of many links:
    • Novels -> Links to my longer works of fiction. I’ve yet to write any.
    • Obsessions -> A collection of things I love. Same as "shrines."
    • Other -> My other type of works: visual, audio, interactive.
    • Shorts -> Links to my short stories and flash fiction.
    • Essays -> Links to my essays and articles.

I also recommend checking out my FAQ page.
