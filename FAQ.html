What is the password for the secret page?

Every once in a while, the riddle shifts, it rearranges itself. Perhaps to offer you more clues or to make a puzzle harder. As a result, the answer to the riddle might change as well. You can’t expect the old keys to work if the keyholes have been altered. Still, I wouldn’t discard any past solution. They may prove useful again later...
