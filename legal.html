My Strict No-Ai Policy

There are few things I despise as much as I despise Ai. This needs not be said, but here it is: my prose will never be written, critiqued, or sniffed by Ai. I’m a serious Luddite when it comes to this. And if I ever betray my own principles may God smite me where I stand!

I write in public. Every time I save a document, Git pushes it straight to this site. This means you can read my works as they’re being written — half-finished sentences, all the typoes, awkward L2 phrasing, and lame, unfinished endings.
What a treat, right?

Another thing: we must restrain Ai wiriting but not our own! It is not the em-dash that makes a text Ai-written. I want to get rid of this disfluenting anxiety and use "—" whenever—and wherever—I—so—damn—please.
