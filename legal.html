My no Ai policy

(I never capitalise the ‘i’, it’s not intelligent.)

There are few things I despise as much as I despise “Ai art.” Everything you see on this website, from the prose, the illustrations, to the layout and markup, are the labour of human minds. No Ai was used in the authoring of this site, with one exception.

Most of the JavaScript is vibe coded. Hypocrisy! I know, I know. My reasoning is simple: I’m not a developer, this is not a dev portfolio. As much as I would like to, I can’t currently justify spending time learning JS. However, the website was made with progressive enhancement in mind, so nearly every page is accessible without JS (or CSS for that matter).

Anonymity > Privacy

Privacy is Marketing. Anonymity is Architecture.

“We and our 382 partners care about your privacy...”

I can’t track you. I can’t collect analytics about who you are, where you’re from, what you read, or how long you stayed. This is by design. I’m not asking you to trust that I won’t sell your data, I’m stating that I don’t have data to sell. Not that I would, anyway.

When using my contact form you may provide an email address. In this case, you are no longer anonymous, but this is entirely your choice. I’ll only use it to reply, I won’t share your email with third parties, and won’t add you to any mailing list for marketing. My reply address is zoltan.forrai@gildrom.com. I retain correspondence as long as necessary to respond to you and maintain records of our conversation.

The contact form relies on formsubmit.co, they have their own privacy policy. Gildrom.com is hosted on GitHub Pages, the domain name is provided by Cloudflare. By using this website, you acknowledge they may collect data according to their own policies.

While this site is designed with anonymity in mind, I cannot guarantee your privacy due to the third-party infrastructure required to deliver the site to you.
