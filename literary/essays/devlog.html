## [0.1.0] 100126 • A COMPLETE REFACTOR OF THE WHOLE WEBSITE

Two years ago I wrote my first line of HTML. These two years have taught me a lot, and I felt it was time to retire the old HTML and CSS, and rewrite the whole site from the bottom up.

Consider this the Beta release of the site. Some pages are still missing but everything rests on solid ground.

Notable changes:

  • Removed bloat and simplified the codebase.
  • Fiction pages received cleaner markup and css. Using oklch color space and nested selectors.
  • Instead of many separate submodules and GitHub sub-repos, the website now lives in a singular monorepo, making migration much simpler if needed.
