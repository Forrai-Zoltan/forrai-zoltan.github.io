Zoltan Forrai

Greetings. The name’s Zoltan, although online many know me by my alias, Gildrom. I’m an English teacher by trade, writer by night, and wizard at heart. Read my lore.

I find many facets of contemporary life quite alienating, and choose to abjure them from my life. I’ve forsaken all social media and made my home here some time ago.

This, is not really a website. It’s a hermitage in the shape of a website. It’s an archive for my writing, my art, and other crimes. I’m certain you’ll find something to enjoy. So go ahead, read some fiction, or find some secrets.
