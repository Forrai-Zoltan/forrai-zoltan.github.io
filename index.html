Forrai Zoltán ~

Hi there! My name is Forrai Zoltán. I'm a scholar living in Budapest, Hungary.

I'm passionate about many different things, from the humanities to technology. I like to think I have what you might call 'can't sit on his ass syndrome', — it's terminal. Not that I'm hyperactive, but I constantly require something to tinker with, to write, draw, or just think about. I often ping pong between a couple of projects, going from one interest to another every few weeks. This keeps my life interesting.

Not all those who wander are lost. — J.R.R. Tolkien

This website was partially built in an attempt to break away from social media, AI slop and web 2.0/3.0 in general. I handwrite the code myself using only HTML and CSS. The reason I don't use any JavaScript is not just because I think it's bloat (it is), but also because I don't know how to actually code. So, do Not check out my GitHub.
