Secret (wip)

Within the site, Answers are hidden and Questions are found.

On the pages are the clues. Some are clear, others confuse.

Many of them point the way. Some, adversely point away.

Some are there for you to stall, and point to nowhere at all.


What can be trusted on a site so herring ridden?

But yet again, somewhere there is a Riddle hidden.

Perhaps on some other page a Key may be found.

Secrets might unveil, if your solution is sound.
