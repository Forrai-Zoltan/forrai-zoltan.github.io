Secret (wip)

Within the site, Answers are hidden and Questions are found.

On the pages are the clues — some are clear, some confuse.

Many of them point the way; some, adversely point away.

Some are there for you to stall, as they point nowhere at all.


What could be true, I ask, inside a site as herring-ridden?

But then again, somewhere in here there is a Riddle hidden.

Perhaps within another page a Key may be found.

Secrets might unveil themselves, if your result is sound.
