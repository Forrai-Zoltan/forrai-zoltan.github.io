Secret (Work in Progress)

A website written with Answers hidden and Questions found.

In the pages are the clues — some are clear, some confuse.

Many of them point the way; some, instead just point away.

Some are there for you to stall, as they point nowhere at all.


What could be true, I ask, inside a site this herring-ridden?

But then again, somewhere in here there is a Riddle hidden.

Perhaps within another page a Key may be found.

Secrets might reveal themselves, if your result is sound.
