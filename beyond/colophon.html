And as the chapter closes, the spell shatters...

Thank you for visiting my site, friend! Did you enjoy your stay? I sincerely hope you were able to find something fun to read. And I hope my puzzles weren’t too difficult to distract from the mystery. Did you find every secret I’ve hidden? Or are you still looking? Well, don’t give up now!

This website is a passion project of mine, and I put a great deal of love and work into it. Every day I find something new to fix or tinker with. Keep in mind, however, that I’m no dev, just a humble English teacher (TEFL) trying his best. It probably goes without saying, but the site is under permanent construction; not everything may work as expected... or at all.

If you did enjoy your time here, would you consider?

  • Bookmarking my page in your browser,
  • Following my RSS feed for news and updates,
  • Linking to me through my button:

I hope to see you again soon. By next time, I’ll have more stories, more riddles, and more magic to share with you. Until then, with the greatest of wishes,

Your loyal friend,

Gildrom Qeeney, aka Forrai Zoltán
